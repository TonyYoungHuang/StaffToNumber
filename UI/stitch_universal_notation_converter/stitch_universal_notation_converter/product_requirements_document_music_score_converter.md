# Product Requirements Document: Music Score Converter (Staff to Jianpu)

## 1. Project Overview
A specialized web application for converting PDF sheet music between Staff Notation (Five-line) and Numbered Musical Notation (Jianpu). The primary focus is generating Jianpu from Staff notation, catering to both the Chinese and international markets.

## 2. Target Audience
*   **China/East Asia:** Musicians and educators who frequently use Jianpu.
*   **Global:** International musicians interested in exploring Jianpu or needing conversion for cross-cultural collaboration.

## 3. Core Features (MVP)
*   **File Upload:** Support for PDF and common image formats of sheet music.
*   **Intelligent Conversion:** AI-driven analysis to translate staff notes into numbered notation.
*   **File Preview:** Side-by-side view of original and converted scores.
*   **File Download:** Export converted scores as high-quality PDF or MusicXML.
*   **Cloud Import:** Integration with Google Drive, Dropbox, and WeChat for easy file access.

## 4. UI/UX Requirements
*   **Hero Section:** Clear value prop: "Convert Music Notation Instantly."
*   **Business Section:** Highlight benefits: Time-saving, accurate, accessible.
*   **Functional/Feature Section:** Step-by-step guide and clear CTAs for the conversion tool.
*   **Localization:** Support for English and Simplified Chinese.
